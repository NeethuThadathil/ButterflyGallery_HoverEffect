2D Transforms

translate()

This moves the element 20 pixels to the right and 100 pixels down.

rotate()

This rotates the element 45 degrees clockwise.

scale()

This scales the element to twice its original size in both width and height

skewX()

This skews the element 30 degrees along the X-axis

skewY()

This skews the element 30 degrees along the Y-axis

matrix()

This metrix allow to rotate, scale, move (translate), and skew elements.

3D Transforms

translate3d()

This moves the element 30 pixels to the right, 100 pixels down, and 20 pixels forward on the Z-axis.

rotate3d()

This rotates the element 90 degrees clockwise in X,Y and Z axis

Scale3d()

The element is scaled 2 times along the X-axis,1.5 times along Y-axis,1 times along Z-axis

Combination Effects

rotateX() and rotateY()

Rotates the element around the X-axis by 360 degrees and rotates the element around the Y-axis by 180 degrees.

scale3d() and skewX()

The element sknew 15 degrees along the Y-axis and scaled x-axis double, Y-axis 1.5 and Z-axis 1

translate and rotate

The element moves the element 100 pixels to the right and 20 pixels down. and it rotate 180 degree along the Y-axis
